Including tips, tricks and information.
What is the style of your photography?

We are story tellers, candid photography - wanting to cpature a moment.

We firmly believe in documenting your day as it happens as opposed to posing everyone the whole day (of course we guide you guys, so no need to feel uncomfortable stressed out about it).

Do you need accomodation for our wedding?

If your wedding is more than 100km away, please allow for a AirBnB in your budget. After shooting the whole day on our feet we appreciate our couples allowing us to check in nearby at AirBnB.

Just remember to discuss with us if your venue is more than 3 - 4 hours away from us. The accomodation time will depend on the travel time.

Accomodation is R800 vis AirBnB and we book ourselves this includes a nights stay and breakfast, we found it doing it this way is the most affordable and easiest on you.

What happens if you are ill and cannot be there on my day?

While we hope that it will never have to come to it, we do have plans in place incase of unforeseen circumstances. We explain this in more detail in our contract but we have a network of fellow photographers to shoot with us or in place of us. You will of course get a say in who the stand in photographer is, as we know that you put a lot of thought into choosing us as your photographers.

Where are you based?

We live in Centurion, nothing fancy, but a place to call home.

Do you guys travel for weddings? How do I budget?

That's an easy one. We most certainly do travel for weddings! We ask R7.50 per kilometer from our home in Centurion. We have our bags packed and ready to go!

How long after our special day do we receive our photos?

The editing process is just as important to us as capturing the moments on your wedding day!

We invest a lot of time in this and ensures that your story is told the best way possible.

Because of this waiting period is usually 8 - 12 weeks. But if we are finished with this before this with your story we will get images to you as fast as possibel.
