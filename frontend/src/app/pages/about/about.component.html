About One Second One Shot

We are story tellers and something we always say is One Second One Shot One Moment this is what we live by making sure that we get that one shot, in that one specific moment you will always want to have around.

We believe in documenting your day as it happens as opposed to posing everyone the whole day (yes we will guide you in a couple of posed photos but no need to feel uncomfortable).

We can never stand still and love being busy, we always try to see things differently taking something ordinary and make it extraordinary. This shows in our work always finding the most creative and artistic way to show your day.
